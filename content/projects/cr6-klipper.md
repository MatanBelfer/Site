Took an older Creality CR-6 and replaced its stock firmware with the latest Klipper, adapting the configuration to the printer's non-standard hardware (including its strain-gauge probing system).

## Highlights

- Full firmware replacement and configuration on hardware Klipper doesn't support out of the box.
- Custom G-code macros extending machine behavior — the same skill set needed to drive custom motion hardware in a lab prototype.
